Frequently Asked Questions

How much does an online consultation cost?

Our standard consultation fee is $30 for a 20-minute session. However, if you're a registered member with an active prescription, your first consultation each month is complimentary.

Can the pharmacist prescribe medication?

Our pharmacists can provide recommendations, but they cannot prescribe new medications. They can, however, help manage existing prescriptions, suggest over-the-counter options, and coordinate with your doctor for prescription needs.

How long does a consultation typically last?

Standard consultations are scheduled for 20 minutes. For more complex health concerns, we offer extended 40-minute sessions at an additional cost.

What if I need to cancel or reschedule?

You can cancel or reschedule your appointment up to 4 hours before the scheduled time without any penalty. For cancellations made less than 4 hours in advance, a 50% fee may apply.
