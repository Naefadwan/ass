Regulatory Compliance

Last Updated: May 12, 2025

Our Commitment to Compliance

At E-Pharma, we are committed to maintaining the highest standards of regulatory compliance in all aspects of our operations. We understand the critical importance of adhering to pharmaceutical regulations and healthcare standards to ensure the safety and well-being of our customers.

This page outlines our compliance with various regulations and standards that govern online pharmacies and telemedicine services.

Pharmacy Licensing

E-Pharma operates in full compliance with all applicable pharmacy laws and regulations. Our pharmacy operations are licensed in all jurisdictions where we provide services.

  • State Board of Pharmacy License #PH-12345
  • National Association of Boards of Pharmacy (NABP) Verified Internet Pharmacy Practice Sites (VIPPS) Program accredited
  • Digital Pharmacy Accreditation from the Accreditation Commission for Health Care (ACHC)

All pharmacists employed by E-Pharma are licensed professionals who adhere to the highest standards of pharmaceutical care.

Telemedicine Compliance

Our telemedicine services comply with all state and federal regulations governing virtual healthcare. We adhere to:

  • American Telemedicine Association (ATA) guidelines
  • Federation of State Medical Boards (FSMB) telemedicine policies
  • State-specific telemedicine regulations

All healthcare providers on our platform are licensed in the states where they provide care and maintain appropriate malpractice insurance coverage.

Privacy and Data Security

E-Pharma is committed to protecting your personal and health information in accordance with:

  • Health Insurance Portability and Accountability Act (HIPAA)
  • Health Information Technology for Economic and Clinical Health (HITECH) Act
  • General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) for our European customers
  • State privacy laws including California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA)

Our systems undergo regular security assessments and penetration testing to ensure the highest level of data protection. For more details, please refer to our Privacy Policy.

Prescription Medication Standards

We adhere to strict protocols for prescription fulfillment:

  • Verification of all prescriptions by licensed pharmacists
  • Strict adherence to prescription drug monitoring program (PDMP) requirements
  • Secure electronic prescribing systems compliant with DEA requirements for controlled substances
  • Comprehensive medication review and drug interaction screening

We do not dispense medications without valid prescriptions from licensed healthcare providers, and we implement multiple verification steps to prevent prescription fraud.

Product Safety and Supply Chain Integrity

E-Pharma maintains rigorous standards to ensure product safety and authenticity:

  • Compliance with the Drug Supply Chain Security Act (DSCSA)
  • All medications sourced from FDA-approved manufacturers and authorized distributors
  • Strict temperature-controlled shipping and storage for temperature-sensitive medications
  • Comprehensive tracking and tracing of all pharmaceutical products

We do not source medications from international pharmacies or distributors not authorized by the FDA.
