Frequently Asked Questions

How do I track my delivery?

Once your order is confirmed, you'll receive a tracking link via email and SMS. You can also track your delivery in real-time through your account dashboard. Our delivery personnel will update the status at each stage of the delivery process.

What if I'm not home during delivery?

If you're not home during delivery, our delivery personnel will follow your delivery preferences set during checkout. Options include: leaving the package in a secure location, delivery to a neighbor, or rescheduling for a more convenient time. For medications requiring signature, we'll leave a notice and attempt delivery again the next day.

How are temperature-sensitive medications handled?

Temperature-sensitive medications are transported in specialized packaging with temperature-controlled insulation and cooling elements. Our delivery vehicles are equipped with refrigeration units when necessary, and we monitor temperature throughout transit to ensure your medications maintain their efficacy.

Can I schedule a specific delivery time?

Yes, you can select a preferred delivery window during checkout. We offer morning (9 AM - 12 PM), afternoon (12 PM - 4 PM), and evening (4 PM - 8 PM) delivery windows. For premium members, we also offer specific 2-hour delivery windows for added convenience.
