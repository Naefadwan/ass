Full Description

Paracetamol is a commonly used over-the-counter pain reliever (analgesic) and fever reducer (antipyretic). It can be used to relieve mild to moderate pain from headaches, muscle aches, toothaches, and menstrual periods. It is also used to reduce fever.

Side Effects

  • Rare side effects include rash
  • Blood disorders
  • Hypersensitivity reactions

Usage

Take one tablet every 4-6 hours as needed. Do not exceed 4000mg in 24 hours.

Warnings

Consult a doctor if symptoms persist or worsen. Keep out of reach of children.
